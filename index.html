📋 What Happens Next?

  1. Submit Request: Fill out the form to request a new school registration.
  2. Admin Review: A Platform Administrator will review your school information and verify your credentials.
  3. Setup Admin Account: Upon approval, your school profile is created, and your coordinator account is granted **School Admin** rights.
