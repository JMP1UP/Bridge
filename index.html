👋 Connect Your School to Bridge

Welcome to the Bridge Onboarding Portal! In order to participate in the international cultural exchange, your school must be registered on the platform.

Request New School Registration

📋 What Happens Next?

  1. Submit Request: Fill out the form to request a new school registration.
  2. Admin Review: A Platform Administrator will review your school information and verify your credentials.
  3. Setup Admin Account: Upon approval, your school profile is created, and your coordinator account is granted **School Admin** rights.
  4. Invite Students: You can then log in, manage settings, and invite your students.
